Castell Project, Inc.

Castell Project, a 501(c)3 nonprofit, provides hospitality companies tools and support to accelerate development of their women executives, the largest component of their workforce and the greatest source of future industry leaders. Castell Project, Inc. delivers impactful and structured leadership development for talented, high potential women executives andongoing training and networking opportunities. Performance metrics to assess and improve performance of Castell Project’s programs are delivered by Georgia State University, academic partner to Castell Project. Castell Project, with Georgia State University, also deliversinnovative research on diversity in the hospitality industry. Castell Project actively seeks sponsors, champions and program participants.

Castell Leadership Program

We can accelerate the advancement of excellent women executives in hospitality leadership with training, advocacy, and a support network. Castell Leadership Program does this with an impactful package including an intensive seminar, web-based training, WILL cohort support, Profiles International 360-evaluation, and individual coaching.
