Why?

Few hospitality industry companies have the internal capacity to offer high potential women top caliber gender focused leadership development. Yet with the right leadership development companies gain loyalty and improved productivity from their most valuable employees.

  • 65% of graduates from college hospitality programs are women. Our talent pipeline is strong.
  • US Department of Labor reports that 59% of hospitality industry employees are women. Women are committed and delivering to the hospitality industry.
  • Rockefeller Foundation found that the presence of women in leadership positions is important to 76% of women in choosing where to work. Women leaders are important to recruiting and retention.
  • McKinsey Global Institute (2015) and Reuters (2014) report stronger performance in companies with women in leadership positions.
