WILL : Women in Lodging Leadership

Graduates of the Program are invited to join Women In Lodging Leadership (WILL), women with shared training and purpose who support each other’s career development.

WILL builds relationships through web-based meetings, calls, gatherings at industry events, and personal interaction. WILL members are a resource for each other and strengthen each woman’s resolve to succeed.

WILL members will “pay it forward” and become Champions for other women executives.
