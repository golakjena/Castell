About Us

Like castell builders, athletes who collaborate to create human towers, Castell, Inc. uses collaboration and training to raise the diverse members of our cohorts to new career heights.

Castell, Inc. recognizes that diversity in corporate leadership strengthens the company and enhances profitability.
